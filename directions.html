Preparation and Cooking Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the rice. It's best to use day-old rice as it has a better texture for frying.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Add another tablespoon of oil to the skillet. Stir-fry your choice of vegetables until they are tender.
  5. Mix the cooked rice with the vegetables. Stir-fry for a few minutes until everything is well combined.
  6. Return the scrambled eggs to the skillet. Mix everything together and season with soy sauce and scallions.
  7. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the rice is heated through.

Serving Suggestions

Serve the egg fried rice hot, garnished with additional scallions if desired. This dish can be enjoyed on its own or as a side to your favorite Asian-inspired main courses. Enjoy your meal!
