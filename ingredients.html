Ingredients to make Egg Fried Rice

  • 2 cups of cooked rice (preferably a day old)
  • 2 tablepoon of vegetables oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
